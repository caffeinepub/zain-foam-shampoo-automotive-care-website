# Zain Foam Shampoo Automotive Care Website

## Overview
A professional, mobile-responsive website for Zain Foam Shampoo, an automotive care brand specializing in car and bike cleaning products.

## Brand Identity
- **Colors**: Black, royal purple, white, and cyan accents
- **Typography**: Modern, clean, bold fonts with masculine, professional appearance
- **Style**: Premium automotive detailing brand aesthetic

## Homepage Structure

### Hero Section
- Main headline: "Extra Foam. Extra Shine. Professional Car Wash at Home."
- Sub-headline: "Premium snow foam shampoo with 24-hour dust resistant protection."
- Two call-to-action buttons:
  - "Call Now – 7778057006" (opens phone dialer)
  - "Become a Dealer" (smooth scrolls to dealer form section)

### Product Section
- Product image showcase displaying the uploaded Zain Foam Shampoo product images
- Layout: Side-by-side display on desktop, responsive slider for mobile users
- Images styled with rounded edges and smooth transitions consistent with brand design
- Product name: "Zain Foam Shampoo"
- Feature list:
  - Extra Snow Foaming Formula
  - 24 Hours Dust Resistant Protection
  - pH Neutral & Paint Safe
  - Safe for Glossy & Matte Paint
  - Suitable for Cars, Bikes & SUVs

### How It Works Section
Three-step process:
1. Mix shampoo with water
2. Apply foam using sponge or foam cannon
3. Rinse & enjoy shine

### Why Choose Zain Section
- Content: "Professional grade formula for premium automotive detailing."

### Become a Dealer Section
- Heading: "Become a Dealer"
- Subtext: "Join our network and start selling Zain Foam Shampoo. Fill out the form below."
- Contact form with fields:
  - Name (required)
  - Email (required, with email validation)
  - Phone (required, with phone validation)
- Submit button styled with brand theme

## Technical Features
- Mobile-responsive design
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Subtle hover animations
- Fast loading performance
- SEO optimization
- Form validation for dealer registration
- Responsive image slider for mobile product showcase

## Backend Requirements
The backend must store:
- Dealer registration form submissions with name, email, and phone number
- Form submission timestamps

Backend operations:
- Accept and validate dealer registration form data
- Store dealer inquiries for business follow-up
